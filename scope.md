# Project Purpose
I am making this project to help people that are looking into honest Sales and Trades, Someone looking to get into collecting. Something like retro consoles, TCG games, board games, even books. This is who I am thinking about when it comes to this. They want to start, but dont know how or where to find anything. This webpage will be for them. 
# Description
* Being able to start and show off your collection.
* Being able to speak with anyone and everyone about theirs. 
* Use this platform to get information or locations about something you are currently looking for. 
* Use this site to learn how to effectivly network with others and work on getting something you need. 
# Desired Result
There are a number of things I wish to be the desired result. 
1. Being able to have someone finish their collection. 
2. Helping someone start a collection. 
3. Being to learn or to "Haggle" and have some experience in selling. 
# Exclusions
The only thing that should be excluded from this is online stuff. Accounts (steam, epic).
